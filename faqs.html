What about my pets?

Pets are welcome. If your pet is anxious around new people, consider keeping them in a separate room during the clean.

Who does JMROWLAND send to clean my home?

We send trained, vetted cleaners with clear checklists and quality standards. Team size depends on your space and service type.

Do I need to tip my cleaning technician?

Tipping is optional. If you’re happy with the service, a tip is appreciated but never expected.

What if my cleaning falls on a holiday?

We’ll confirm availability ahead of time. If rescheduling is needed, we’ll offer the closest available date.

Will the initial cleaning take longer?

Often yes — first visits usually involve deeper detailing to bring the space to a baseline standard.

Do I need to be home while my house is being cleaned?

Not required. Many clients share access instructions. We can also clean while you’re home — whichever you prefer.

Will you send the same cleaning technician each time?

We try to keep consistency when possible. If scheduling changes, we’ll still follow the same checklist and quality process.

How do I pay for my cleaning service?

Payment options can include mobile money, cash, or bank transfer depending on your preference. We’ll confirm at booking.
